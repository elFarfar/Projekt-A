Producers

We’re dedicated to helping producers across all genres release their music at the highest quality. With NOTE, you won’t have to worry about the technical details. We connect you to top-tier mastering services at no upfront cost to you. Simply send us your tracks, and we’ll take care of the mastering process, ensuring your music shines. How It Works:

  1. Submit Your Tracks: Send us your music for mastering.
  2. Set Your Prices: You decide the selling price of your tracks.
  3. Get an Estimated Value: We provide an estimated market value for your music to guide your pricing.
  4. Profit Sharing: We only take a 15% commission on all sales, ensuring you earn from your passion.
  5. Once your tracks are mastered, we promote your music to artists and listeners alike, helping you connect with the right audience. Let NOTE transform your creative vision into a reality!
